Telecom Licensing in Lebanon

In Lebanon, the Telecommunications Regulatory Authority (TRA) categorizes licenses for the provision of telecommunications services into three types: Restricted Licenses, Individual Licenses, and Class Licenses. Each of these licenses follows a different procedure outlined in the Licensing Regulation, based on specific criteria for licensing, service provision, and facility operation.

Restricted Licenses:

Number: Limited in quantity.
Qualifications: A legal entity registered in Lebanon that meets specific qualifications designed for the licenses.
Applications: Submit applications following the Request for Applications (RFA) prepared and published by the Authority.
Competitive Process: Requires a competitive process for license acquisition.
Authority Approval: Requires approval from the Authority, and for services covered by Article 19(1), it also requires a proposal from the Minister and a decree from the Council of Ministers.
Right to Build Facilities: Typically includes the right to build facilities.
Roll-out and Coverage Obligations: May include roll-out and coverage obligations.

Individual Licenses:

Number: Unlimited in quantity.
Qualifications: A legal entity registered in Lebanon that meets non-competitive, minimum eligibility criteria.
Applications: Can be submitted at any time without following a specific RFA.
Competitive Process: No competitive process is required.
Authority Approval: Requires approval from the Authority.
License Issuance: The license is considered issued if the application is not rejected within 30 days.
Right to Build Facilities: Not automatically included.
Roll-out and Coverage Obligations: Not included.

Class Licenses:

Qualifications: Lebanese residents or legal entities registered in Lebanon.
List of Licenses: Includes various licenses such as ISP, Value Added Services, Resale of services, Wireless zone Internet access, Satellite telephony user, and more.
Right to Build Facilities: Not automatically included.
Roll-out and Coverage Obligations: Not included.
